Pro Tips

  • Use a unique password for every account — never reuse.
  • 16+ characters is ideal for most accounts.
  • Passphrases (4 random words) are easier to remember and very secure.
  • Store passwords in a trusted password manager (e.g. Bitwarden, 1Password).
  • Always enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) where possible.

Why CSPRNG?

JavaScript's Math.random() is not cryptographically secure. SentinelPass uses Python's secrets module which draws from the OS entropy pool (/dev/urandom on Linux/macOS, CryptGenRandom on Windows) for true unpredictability.
